Privacy Policy for Anonnote

Effective Date: 19/04/2026

Anonnote respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

We may collect the following information:

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase services to securely store data. Your information is protected using industry-standard security measures.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by trusted services such as Google Firebase to provide app functionality.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
